### How It Works

1. **Create Account**: Set a strong master password (never stored on servers)
2. **Add Credentials**: Store passwords, cards, notes, and identities
3. **Auto-fill**: Click the key icon on login forms to fill credentials
4. **Sync**: Your encrypted data syncs across all your devices

### Privacy Promise

- Your master password is never transmitted
- All encryption happens on your device
- Server only stores encrypted blobs
- No tracking, no analytics, no data mining
